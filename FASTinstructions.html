FAST Music Visualisation Instructions

Welcome to the FAST music visualiser.
This app visualises timbre and pitch/key for a specified music clip.

When the page first loads you'll see the rows of data that make up the pitch and timbre of the track. The track name is shown in the top left corner. There are 12 dimensions for a track's timbre which are shown in red in the visualisation and 12 pitches/keys shown below. A transparent plane along with an arrow shows the current playing time of the track in question (don't worry that it's not at the start). The controls on the left of the screen determine how the visualisation is viewed and control the playback of the song.
